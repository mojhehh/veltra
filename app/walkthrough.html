Web Browsing

Veltra includes two ways to browse the web. Ultraviolet lets you access sites that may be restricted on your network. The built-in browser handles everyday browsing.

Ultraviolet Proxy

Access blocked sites like YouTube, Discord, and social media. Handles logins, video playback, and interactive content.

Personalization

Adjust Veltra to fit your preferences. Change wallpapers, switch themes, configure the taskbar, and set up tab cloaking for privacy.

Settings

Change your wallpaper, clock format, taskbar position, and other display preferences.

Tab Cloaking

Disguise the browser tab so Veltra looks like a different website (e.g. Google Classroom).
