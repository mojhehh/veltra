Help

Need assistance? Start here.

Fastest paths

  • Help form: just make a issue on our github, one of us will get back to you soon!
  • Q&A: Same as above
  • Direct contact: community managers list their Discord handles on the README.

What to include

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened vs. what you expected
  • Steps to reproduce (screenshots or short clips help a lot)
  • Your browser + OS version

House rules

  • Be respectful and constructive
  • No malicious payloads or unsafe links
  • Keep personal info out of public issues
